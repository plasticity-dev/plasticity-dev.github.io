About Us

Where we started to where we are now.

  • 2008

    A Friendship

    Our co-founders met in middle school and immediately bonded over creative aspirations and goals of becoming famous musicians. A frienship like this was born to last, and nothing was going to stop them from doing awesome things together.

  • 2012

    A Three-Year Spirit Walk

    Life happens and people take their seperate paths. Sean moved to San Francisco to study photograpy and Jon went to Ohio University to study Sociology & Psychology. They had their own experiences and never once thought their friendship as dimished.

  • 2015

    A Series of Conversations

    Through a series of cross-country moves, our co-founders found themselves living together in Chicago. Over the course of a few months, some serious conversations about making a name for themselves arose. These were talks about what people want, how they can provide that, and what they wanted to do. Design and development were at the top of the list.

  • 2016

    Plasticity is Born

    A name. Plasticity is the quality of being easily changed, molded. It's the adapability of something to cater to it's surroundings in order to be the best it can be. That was what Sean and Jon decided they wanted the essence of their company to behold. A product and practice capable of duress and change, while maintaining quality and care.
